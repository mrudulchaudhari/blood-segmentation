About Anemia

Anemia is a condition characterized by low red blood cell count or reduced hemoglobin. In microscopic analysis, anemic blood typically shows:

  • Reduced number of red blood cells
  • Pale or smaller red blood cells
  • Irregular cell distribution

About Leukemia

Leukemia is a type of blood cancer. Under microscopic examination, leukemic blood may show:

  • Abnormal white blood cell shapes
  • Higher count of immature white blood cells
  • Irregular cell morphology
